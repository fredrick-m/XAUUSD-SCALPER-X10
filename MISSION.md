# MISSION — XAUUSD-SCALPER-X10

## Objective

Find repeatable scalping strategies on XAUUSD M1 capable of **10x account growth in less than 20 trading days**, validated a minimum of 5 times across multiple market regimes.

## Validation Criteria

| Metric | Requirement |
|---|---|
| Win Rate | > 62% |
| Profit Factor | > 2.0 |
| Max Drawdown | < 35% |
| x10 Target | Achieved minimum 5 times on historical data |
| Market Regimes | Tested on minimum 3 (trend, range, high volatility) |

## Success Definition

A strategy is considered **validated** when all five criteria above are met simultaneously across at least 3 distinct market regime periods. The x10 target must be achieved a minimum of 5 separate times — not once across a long run, but 5 independent instances.

## Constraints

- Timeframe: XAUUSD M1 (1-minute bars)
- Instrument: Gold vs USD (spot or CFD)
- Growth target: x10 in ≤ 20 trading days per validated run
- No curve-fitting: strategies must generalize across regimes

## North Star

We are not looking for a strategy that worked once. We are looking for a repeatable edge — one that survives regime changes, stress tests, and forward walk-throughs. If it cannot do x10 five times on historical data across three market regimes, it is not the answer.
